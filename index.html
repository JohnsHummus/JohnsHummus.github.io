Recipe #1: Twinkie Weiner Sandwich from Weird Al's UHF

  • Time: 1-2 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Twinkie 1
Hot dog 1
Spray cheese 1 can

Step 1: Remove a solo twinkie from the wrapping, turn it upside down and make an incision down the middle (don't cut through).

Step 2: Place a hot dog in the previously made incision of the twinkie.

Step 3: Top the hot dog with your spray cheese in somehow find pleasure in this beautiful mess.

Recipe #2: Troy's Casserole from Community

  • Time: 40 minutes (10 min prep, 30 min cook time)
  • Serves: 8-10 people
Ingredients Quantity
Hot Pockets 6
Pepperoni Pizza Bagel Bites 12-15
Doritos 1 bag

Step 1: Remove the tops of 6 hot pockets and place into a casserole dish. Lay 12-15 pepperoni pizza bagel bites atop your bottom layer of hot pockets.

Step 2: Crumble a bag of Doritos Nacho Cheese chips on top until it covers all of the pizza bites.

Recipe #3: Car Panini from Family Guy

  • Time: 5-10 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
White bread 2 slices
Butter Enough to butter one side of each slice of bread
Vienna sausages 1 can
Doritos 1 bag
Cadbury Egg 1 egg
Reese's Peanut Butter Cups 4 cups (as in chocolate cups)

Step 1: Drain your can of vienna sausages and set them aside.

Step 2: Butter each slice of bread on 1 side.

Step 3: Begin layering your sausages, followed by 4 Reese's Peanut Butter Cups, some crushed Doritos, and 1 Cadbury Egg.

Step 4: Cover with the non buttered side of bread and place on your panini press. Cook until golden brown and the chocolate has melted inside.
